What is it?

We participated in a game jam between colleges and the theme was Halloween. Our idea was not to create any stereotypical pumpkin Halloween but instead make characters rebel against pumpkins. The character has a football as his head. He discovered it works just as well as pumpkin. The scrolling background is gorgeous and exciting. All background assets stay in place, but it seems they are moving. What I also liked in the project was the mono color space complemented with bright vfx and bright details.

Art direction was dark but not black.

Even simple light looks beautiful in dark.

Coding

I mainly focused on the coding and general design. The challenge was to make reusable, easily customizable parts. All the characters share the same scripts and their behaviour is customized with scriptable objects. Time to make the game was short, so we had to cut some corners and copy pasta a lot.

Enemies and boss were essentially same thing just customized differently.

Vfx effects are important in dark setting.
