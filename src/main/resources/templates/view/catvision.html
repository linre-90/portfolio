Catvision

27.12.2022

Catvision is another interactive addition to the blog. It presents visual differences between the cat's POV and human POV.

Overview of the game

What is it?

Catvision presents and plays on the knowledge that cats see the world very differently. It is a simulation about how cats might see the world. Graphics and animations are not realistic, and they are clunky. It was not the main focus. Main focus was to get a somewhat realistic visible difference between, cat's and human visions. The simulation game is of course filled with cat knowledge.

The whole point was to demonstrate differences between humans and cats and their points of view.

Goals and lifespan

I already had some prior experience with Unity game engine. Goal was not to learn new stuff (but that happened too), it was to make a simulation based on data and theories. It is quite heavy to run thanks to the great amount of post-processing. Webgl build was something completely new for me and I did not have any experience with that part of Unity, but it was pretty straight forward. Application is still available over here.
